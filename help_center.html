Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I place an order?

Browse our collections, choose your desired items, and add them to your cart. Go to checkout and complete your order.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit and debit cards, as well as PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

How can I track my order?

Once shipped, a tracking number will be emailed to you. Use it to track your order under “Track My Order.”

Can I change or cancel my order?

Orders can be changed or canceled within 24 hours. Contact support as soon as possible for changes.

What is the return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy on most items. Visit our Return Policy page for details.
